WELCOME TO Djahid Hospital

Secure your spot with our specialists at Unity Hospital. Booking an appointment is just a few clicks away. Follow the link below, select your preferred department and doctor, and choose a time that suits you. Once booked, you'll receive confirmation of your appointment date and time.

At Unity Hospital, our cutting-edge triage system revolutionizes the way we manage patient care. Upon booking an appointment, each patient is assigned a unique reference number, which is then used to navigate our triage process. This system meticulously evaluates the urgency of each case by considering the severity of symptoms and the duration the patient has been waiting. As patients check in, our automated system updates their position in real-time, ensuring that those in urgent need receive immediate attention, while others are kept informed of their wait time. This seamless integration of technology and healthcare ensures efficient, transparent, and compassionate care for every individual who walks through our doors.
